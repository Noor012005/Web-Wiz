Our Mission

"Every animal deserves safety, care, and a loving home."

At Paws & Hope, we believe in a world where no animal suffers from neglect or abuse. Founded in 2018, our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to rescue abandoned animals from the streets of Mumbai and surrounding areas.

Our approach is comprehensive - from emergency medical care to rehabilitation and adoption programs, we're committed to giving every animal a second chance at happiness.

Government certified animal welfare organization since 2019

The Journey to a Forever Home

Day 1

The Rescue

Luna was found trembling beneath a bridge during monsoon rains. Malnourished and with an injured paw, she cowered when our rescue team approached.

25% of journey complete

Day 3

Medical Care

Our vet team discovered Luna had a fractured leg and severe malnutrition. Treatment began immediately with antibiotics, pain management, and a special diet to help her gain strength.

50% of journey complete

Week 2

Rehabilitation

After her physical wounds began healing, Luna's emotional recovery started in a foster home. Gentle socialization, training, and consistent love helped her begin to trust humans again.

75% of journey complete

Month 1

Forever Home

The Sharma family fell in love with Luna during a shelter visit. After a thorough home check and adoption process, Luna found her forever family, complete with a loving home and an 8-year-old girl who became her best friend.

Journey complete! ❤️

Buddy's Miracle

Hit by a car and left paralyzed, Buddy regained mobility through intensive hydrotherapy. Now he works as a therapy dog, bringing joy to children in hospitals.

"Seeing children's faces light up when Buddy visits is incredible. He's been through so much pain himself, yet he brings such happiness to others. He's not just my pet, he's my hero."

Featured Story

Bella: From Streets to Stardom

When we found Bella, she was severely malnourished and terrified of human touch. After weeks of patient care and rehabilitation, she not only recovered physically but blossomed into a confident, loving dog.

"Bella taught us that every animal, no matter how broken they seem, has an incredible capacity for recovery and love when given a chance."

Today, Bella serves as a therapy dog, visiting hospitals and nursing homes, bringing comfort to those in need. Her journey represents everything our shelter stands for – second chances and the transformative power of compassion.
